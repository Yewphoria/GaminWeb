FAQ

What are Garmin's shipping options?
Garmin offers express free shipping for all orders.
How long does delivery take for Garmin products?
Delivery times for Garmin products depend on your location and product availability. You'll receive an estimated delivery date during the checkout process.
Can I change my delivery address or modify/cancel my order if it hasn't shipped yet?
Yes, you can modify/cancel your order or change the delivery address if the order hasn't shipped. Contact customer support promptly to make changes.
How do I track my Garmin order and check its delivery status?
To track your order, use the provided tracking number in the email sent after the purchase.
What is Garmin's return policy?
Garmin has a 14-days return policy for products in their original condition, unused, and with all original tags and packaging.
How do I return my Garmin order?
Changes can be made before shipping. Please contact our customer support for assistance.
When will I receive my refund for a returned Garmin product?
Refunds are processed as soon as possible, with the timeframe typically ranging from 1 to 2 weeks. The exact duration depends on your bank's processing times.
How do I find the right size and fit for Garmin products?
Garmin provides sizing guides and product specifications on its website to help you choose the right size and fit for your needs. Refer to sizing charts before making a purchase.
Do Garmin watches have a warranty?
Yes, Garmin watches come with a 1-year warranty that covers manufacturing defects and non-human caused damages. Refer to our warranty policy for specific details.
What do I do if my questions isn't covered here?
If your question isn't addressed in our FAQs, please reach out to our customer support team . Alternatively, you can visit a Garmin store for in-person assistance and personalized service.
