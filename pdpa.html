Privacy Policy for Garmin

Last Updated: [11 November 2023]

At Garmin, safeguarding your privacy is our top priority. This Privacy Policy outlines our meticulous processes for collecting and handling your personal data, specifically concerning checkout procedures, providing details for customer support, and scheduling appointments with our service center.

Categories of Personal Data Processed by Garmin

1. Checkout Information:

During purchases, we collect essential details such as name, address, email, and payment information.
The legal ground for this is to facilitate order processing and fulfillment of the agreement between the user and Garmin during the purchase process.

2. Customer Support:

Engaging with our support services involves collecting information, including name, address, phone number, email, and specifics regarding the product or issue. The legal ground for this processing is our legitimate interest in providing quality customer support.

3. Service Center Appointments:

Booking appointments requires providing particulars like name, contact information, and detailed product information.
The legal ground for this is Garmin's legitimate interest in ensuring effective appointment management for the convenience of our users.

4. Newsletter:

If you provide your opt-in consent to receiving marketing information from us, we will also process your email address for the purpose of sending you marketing information about our products, services, and apps, as well as newsletters. The legal ground for processing your email address for this purpose is your consent. You may withdraw your consent at any time by changing your preferences in your account or through the unsubscribe link at the bottom of our marketing emails. The marketing emails you receive from us are based on the preferences you provide in your account, the locale indicated by your Internet Protocol (IP) address, the types of Garmin devices you have added to your account, and any subscriptions included in your account. The legal ground for processing this data for this purpose is our legitimate interest in reducing the number of marketing emails sent to each particular customer by selecting which customers receive a particular marketing email rather than sending every marketing email to every customer who has consented to receiving marketing emails. If you reside in mainland China, we may use your mobile phone number to send you marketing communications via SMS.

Categories of Recipients of Personal Data

1. Service Providers:

Third-party entities may assist in order fulfillment, communication processes, and analytical endeavors.
Illustration: Payment processors, shipping services.

2. Garmin Service Center:

Details related to appointments are conveyed to our service center to facilitate seamless scheduling and preparation.
Exemplification: Internal Garmin service teams.

3. Other Disclosures:

Data may be disclosed to comply with legal requisites or for investigative purposes.
Exemplification: Legal proceedings, fraud prevention.

International Transfers of Personal Data

Your data may be transmitted to Garmin-owned entities globally for support and service.
Safeguards: Adherence to stringent data protection regulations.

Children

We refrain from purposefully gathering data from users under 13 (U.S.) or 16 (rest of the world) without the explicit consent of parents or guardians.

Privacy Policy Updates

Regular updates are conducted, with material alterations being communicated through pertinent channels.

Retention of Personal Data

Data retention aligns with the necessity for specified purposes or as mandated by applicable laws.
Deletion Option: Easily accessible through user requests or the Account Management Center.

Automated Decision-Making

No automated decisions are made that significantly impact users.

Data Controller and Data Protection Officer

For inquiries pertaining to data, contact details are available based on respective regions.

Manage Your Data

Users retain the ability to seamlessly manage their data by reaching out to our customer support.



Cookie Policy for Garmin

Introduction

Cookies are small files stored on your device when you visit a website. Cookies can perform various functions, such as remembering your information, supporting shopping cart functionality, and analyzing site usage. This policy covers cookies and similar technologies used by Garmin on its websites. Please note that when we refer to "cookies," it includes similar technologies like pixels, web beacons, or clear GIFs.

Learn More About Cookies

If you want to learn more about cookies in general, you can visit informational websites, such as allaboutcookies.org or aboutcookies.org.

Our Use of Cookies

We use cookies for the following purposes:

1. Facilitating Site Functionality: Providing you with the best possible experience on our websites.

2. Identifying and Fixing Problems: Analyzing and resolving issues with the website.

3. Analyzing Site Usage: Compiling aggregated statistics to understand user behavior and improve our services.

4. Remembering Your Preferences: Enhancing your experience by remembering your preferences.

5. Preventing Fraudulent Activity: Detecting and preventing fraudulent activity on our websites.

6. Securing the Website and Your Data: Enhancing the security of the website and protecting your data.

7. Understanding Marketing Effectiveness: Analyzing the effectiveness of marketing campaigns and improving SEO.

We collect information, directly or through third-party providers, including:

Network or Connection Information: Such as your IP address.
Device Information: Such as your browser type, language, time zone, operating system, or location.
Usage Information: Such as the time and date of your visit, products purchased, or page views.

Third-Party Services

We use third-party services to facilitate cookies and monitor website performance, including Tealium IQ Tag Management, Google Tag Manager, TrustArc for cookie management, and others for performance analysis and affiliate marketing programs.

Categories of Cookies

Required Cookies: Necessary for basic site functions, such as recognizing sign-in credentials or updating the shopping cart. Most are session cookies.
Functional Cookies: Aid in site analysis and performance, remembering location or language preferences. Some persist for a longer period.
Advertising Cookies: Facilitate advertising on partner websites, personalize ads, and evaluate marketing campaign effectiveness.

Managing Your Cookie Preferences

On Our Website: Click "Cookie Preferences" in the bottom left corner of our website to accept, decline, or manage preferences.
Through Your Browser: Adjust browser settings to accept, reject, or prompt for cookies. Note that blocking all cookies may affect site functionality.
Through Third Parties: Opt-out of targeted advertising by using provided links.

Further Information

For more information or assistance, contact our third-party providers, review our privacy policies, or refer to our contact details for inquiries.

Updates to the Cookie Policy

This Cookie Policy may be updated periodically to reflect improvements, technological changes, or legal requirements. Refer to the "Last Updated" legend for the latest revision date.
